About Me

Brett O'Connell is a 3L at the Loyola Uniersity Chicago School of Law. Brett developed a passion for progressive advocacy early in his burgeoning political career, externing udner the Honorable Judge Jerry Esrig in the Law Division of the Cook County Circuit Court.

Brett has demonstrated an early interest in the field of data privacy law. With the advent of the GDPR and the impending impact of massive data breaches in the United States and abroad, Brett believes that more attorneys and developers alike will be required to serve the interests of data subjects and protect the rights of consumers.
